About Me

I’m Junhao Jiang, you can also call me Aaron.

I am a second-year student at Wilfrid Laurier University. And I have just transferred to User Experience Design this year.

In the first year, I studied Business and Technology Management. UX is a totally new area for me. I like it very much. Compared with the rigid knowledge in BTM, I prefer the study environment here. A lot of the content in the class is inspiring. It will not tell you what you have to do it. Is use what you could or should do it instead.

I really like what Apple’s founder Steve Jobs said about design. It roughly means that people won’t realize what they need until you launch a good product to them. I think this is a very meaningful passage about UX design work. A good designer can find the needs of the user before the user finds them on themselves. And try to improve and solve it.

I personally like to go gym and take a walk along the Grand River, which can effectively relax my body and relieve the pressure of usual tension.
