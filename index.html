Decided to study abroad after graduating from high school. It is a brave decision. From China to Canada, to here WLU. Practice my English level, and experience a different cultural. When I first time took a go train from Brantford to Union Station, my wallet fell on the train and a young couples gave it back to me. The smell of marijuana is everywhere, I think it’s a bad thing, even though it’s legal in Canada now. My head down on the roller coaster, at that moment the world and you are upside down. Ticket and fast line price are so expensive The Brantford YMCA is a gym for students on the WLU Brantford campus. The gym was not built when I first came to this campus. I experienced the experience of the YMCA building from the beginning to the completion. Experienced the facilities in YMCA, very complete and new learn more..
